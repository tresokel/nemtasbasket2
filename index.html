More than a Team, We're Family

Nemtas Basketball Club is dedicated to developing student-athletes of character. We provide a structured, competitive, and supportive environment where players hone their basketball skills while learning invaluable life lessons.

All practices and home games are held at the SMKN 6 South Tangerang Sports Hall. We take pride in our facilities and the incredible support from our school community.
